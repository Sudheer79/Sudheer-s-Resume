RESUME

  • SUMMARY

    • A skilled Web Developer with expertise in designing, developing, and maintaining responsive and user-friendly websites. Proficient in front-end and back-end technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, and databases like MySQL and MongoDB. Experienced in optimizing website performance, ensuring cross-browser compatibility, and implementing security best practices. Passionate about creating seamless digital experiences and continuously learning new technologies to enhance web functionality.

  • EDUCATION

    • To become a **Web Developer**, a **Bachelor’s degree** in Computer Science, IT, or Web Development is preferred but not mandatory. Start by learning **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**, followed by front-end frameworks like **React.js, Angular, or Vue.js**. Master back-end technologies such as **Node.js, Python, PHP, or Java**, along with databases like **MySQL, PostgreSQL, or MongoDB**. Gain full-stack development skills with **MERN (MongoDB, Express.js, React, Node.js)** or **MEAN (MongoDB, Express.js, Angular, Node.js)** stacks. Learn **Git and GitHub** for version control and explore web hosting and deployment platforms like **Netlify, Vercel, AWS, or Firebase**. Study **web security best practices, performance optimization, and SEO techniques** to enhance website functionality. Earning certifications like **Google UX Design, Meta Front-End Developer, or AWS Developer** can boost your credentials. Work on real-world projects such as **portfolio websites, blogs, e-commerce sites, or dashboards** to build experience. Participate in **internships, open-source contributions, and freelance work** to enhance your practical knowledge and career prospects.

  • WORK EXPERIENCE

    • Work experience requirements for a **Web Developer** vary based on the role and company, but most employers prefer candidates with **1-3 years of hands-on experience** in web development. Entry-level positions may accept candidates with **internships, freelance projects, or open-source contributions** as proof of practical skills. Mid-level roles typically require proficiency in **front-end and back-end technologies**, along with experience in **frameworks like React.js, Angular, or Vue.js, and server-side technologies like Node.js, Python, or PHP**. Experience with **databases (MySQL, MongoDB, PostgreSQL), version control (Git/GitHub), APIs, and cloud deployment (AWS, Firebase, or Vercel)** is often required. Strong problem-solving skills, familiarity with **agile development methodologies**, and the ability to work in **cross-functional teams** are highly valued. Senior-level positions generally demand **5+ years of experience**, leadership capabilities, and expertise in **scalability, performance optimization, and security best practices**.

  • SKILLS

    • Relevant skills for a Web Developer include proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for front-end development, along with frameworks like React. js, Angular, or Vue.js. Strong back-end knowledge in Node.js, Python, PHP, Java, or Ruby, and database management with MySQL, PostgreSQL, or MongoDB is essential. Experience with RESTful APIs, GraphQL, and authentication methods enhances back-end capabilities. Proficiency in version control (Git, GitHub), responsive design, UI/UX principles, and cross-browser compatibility is crucial for building user-friendly applications. Knowledge of web security best practices, performance optimization, and SEO techniques is highly beneficial. Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, Firebase, Vercel), CI/CD pipelines, Docker, and Kubernetes adds value. Strong problem-solving skills, debugging expertise, teamwork, and an agile development mindset are key for success in the field.

  • MY CERTIFICATIONS

    • Relevant Web Development certifications include the Meta Front-End Developer Certificate and Google UX Design Professional Certificate for front-end skills. For back-end and full-stack development, certifications like the Meta Back-End Developer Certificate, IBM Full Stack Cloud Developer Certificate, and Node.js Certified Developer (JSNAD) are valuable. Cloud-based certifications such as AWS Certified Developer – Associate, Google Associate Cloud Engineer, and Microsoft Certified: Azure Developer Associate enhance deployment skills. The Certified Kubernetes Application Developer (CKAD) and CIW Web Development Professional certifications help with advanced development and containerization. Earning these certifications strengthens expertise in web technologies, security, and cloud integration.
